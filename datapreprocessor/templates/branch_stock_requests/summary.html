Branch Stock Requests

The branch stock requests are the requests made by chefs, kitchen manager etc. to the backend store owners. The requests are based on the current requirements of the kitchens and it takes some while to get granded from the backend store.

The primary issues faced by branch stock requests are forecasting the quantity of raw materials and reduce the delivery time of raw materials to the kitchen.
